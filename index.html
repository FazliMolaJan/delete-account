Delete Your Account & Data

We understand that sometimes you may wish to delete your Islam Master account and all associated data. To do so, please follow the steps below.

Send an email to:
support@islammaster.com
with the subject line: Delete My Account

In your email, please include your account email address to verify your identity.

Once we receive your request, we will delete:

  • Your account and login details
  • All your questions and associated data
  • Any personal data stored by us
Some community contributions (like public questions) may remain in an anonymous form to benefit others.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to our support.
